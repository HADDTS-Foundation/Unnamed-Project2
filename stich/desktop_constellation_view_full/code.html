NCBI/RefSeq Summary

This gene encodes a protein that binds to the C-terminus of adenovirus E1A proteins. This phosphoprotein is a transcriptional repressor and may also play a role during cellular proliferation.

UniProt Function

  • Corepressor targeting diverse transcription regulators.
  • Functions in brown adipose tissue (BAT) differentiation.
  • NADH-dependent dehydrogenase activity.
